Ph.D. Student at CSE, HKUST

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(HKUST), co-supervised by Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai. I am now studying instance segmentation and 3D scene reconstruction. Prior to that, I received my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Wuhan University in 2018.
